The power of painting

About Us


Are you someone looking for inspiration and guidance? Look no further than the beautiful paintings created on this site! On this site, we showcase the progress of a beginner as she creates paintings. From initial sketches to final touches, you'll see how her skills evolve and improve over time. But this site isn't just about showcasing pretty pictures. We also offer tips and tricks for beginners looking to improve their own art skills. Whether you're struggling with color mixing or unsure of where to start with composition, we've got you covered. So grab your paintbrushes and get ready to be inspired. With a little practice and dedication, you too can create perfect paintings that are sure to impress.

Objective/Goal


Our main objective is to entertain you by making paintings that you love. Our process videos, art, photos, and tutorials, will help you become a better artist and will help you stay entertained.
